How to get computer and touchscreen sinked up:
1. Search your computer for "Displays"
2. Check "Mirror Displays"
3. Change Rotation to "Clockwise"
4. Go to: 
      https://www.thefanclub.co.za/how-to/how-ubuntu-1204-touchscreen-calibration
   and follow steps 1-3
5. Log off and log back in
6. The touch screen should now work (but the mouse/click input may not line up between the computer screen and touchscreen)
